# Melo — 4-Week Social Calendar (Evergreen)

Generated 2026-05-19 from the assets in `~/Downloads/social images/`.
Pairs the carousels, feed posts, stories, and highlight covers with
specific days/platforms so you can post consistently for ~4 weeks
without making anything new.

**Evergreen** — not tied to a release. Reusable any month: just reset
the dates and run it again.

Companion to `marketing/social-schedule.md` (the cadence framework +
hashtag sets + Reddit rules) and `marketing/canva-content-calendar.md`
(briefs for any net-new Canva graphics).

---

## ⚠️ Before you post anything — read this

1. **No Google Play badges.** Melo is iOS-only. `melo_onbrand_02_wrapped.png`
   has a Google Play badge on it — **do not post that one.** Spot-check
   every image before posting; if one shows a Google Play badge,
   Magic-Eraser it out or skip the image.
2. **Prefer the `_fixed` variants.** Where an asset has both a plain
   and a `_fixed` version (e.g. `melo_onbrand_04_festivals` vs
   `melo_onbrand_04_fixed`), the `_fixed` one is the cleaned-up cut —
   use it.
3. **Reels are the Monday slot.** This image folder is stills only —
   the weekly Monday Reel is something you record + edit yourself.
   Full scripts, tools, and the how-to are in
   `marketing/reels-guide.md`. The reel keyframes (`melo_reel1-6_kf`)
   work as cover frames.

## What you're NOT doing this month

- **Not posting any image with a Google Play badge.** iOS-only.
- No "buy now / download now" CTAs in Reddit post bodies — auto-flagged.
- No more than 5-7 hashtags per post — Instagram nerfs spam-stuffed
  captions.
- No new content creation — you have everything you need for 4 weeks.
- No cold-DMing strangers — community-building on Reddit beats it.

## Beyond Week 4

**If a post hits big** (10x your normal reach): drop the calendar and
make 3 more in that style this week. Algorithms reward momentum.

**Month 2 asset pool** — the calendar above uses ~25 of the ~130
images. Everything unused is your next month's supply: `melo_c3_01-08`,
`melo_feat_01-06`, the rest of `melo_batch2_*`, `melo_social_4/5`,
`melo_fixed_02_rankings`, `melo_new_*`, `melo_nomock_02/04`,
`melo_square_1-2`, and the `melo_appstore_*` set (works as "now on the
App Store" proof posts). Re-run this same calendar structure with those.

**If you ship a new feature** — that week becomes a launch week:
replace the Mon/Wed/Fri slots with feature-specific posts and follow
the launch playbook in `social-schedule.md`.

**Reels** — the Monday Reel is the most important post of each week.
The 9-concept library, recording steps, and editing workflow are all
in `marketing/reels-guide.md`. After Week 4, rotate through the
remaining concepts in that guide.
