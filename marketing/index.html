How it works

Three steps to the year you'll want to share.

  1. 1

    Log a show

    Type the artist, pick the date — Melo auto-fills the venue and the actual setlist from Setlist.fm. Drop in your phone shots, score it out of 10.

  2. 2

    Wishlist artists

    Save the bands you'd kill to see live. Melo watches Ticketmaster every day and pings you the moment a show near you announces.

  3. 3

    Open your Wrapped

    Every December, swipe through a story of your year — top artist, top venue, the highest-rated night, the songs you can't stop hearing.
